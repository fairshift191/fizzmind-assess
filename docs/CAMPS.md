# Fizzmind — Camp Locations

## Overview

Fizzmind operates in **9 locations across 6 continents**. Each camp has its own personality shaped by the city, culture, and environment — but all share the same Fizzmind philosophy, cohort size (20), and daily rhythm.

| Location | Country | Duration | Season |
|----------|---------|----------|--------|
| California | USA | 3 weeks | June - July |
| New York | USA | 3 weeks | July - August |
| Dubai | UAE | 2 weeks | June |
| Kuala Lumpur | Malaysia | 2 weeks | July |
| Singapore | Singapore | 2 weeks | June |
| London | UK | 2 weeks | July |
| Paris | France | 2 weeks | July - August |
| Berlin | Germany | 2 weeks | August |
| Barcelona | Spain | 2 weeks | August |

### Age Range
All locations: **10 - 14 years old**

### Cohort Size
All locations: **20 students per cohort**

### Supervision Ratio
1 counselor per 5 students (4 counselors per cohort), plus 1 lead counselor and 1 local coordinator.

### Accommodation
All camps provide supervised residential accommodation (dormitory or hotel-style, gender-separated, 2-4 kids per room). Meals included (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks). Dietary requirements accommodated.

## California, USA

**Duration:** 3 weeks
**Vibe:** Innovation meets nature. Silicon Valley energy mixed with Pacific coast adventure.

### Location & Facilities
Based near the San Francisco Bay Area with access to tech campus visits, coastal trails, and dedicated maker spaces. Campus-style accommodation with outdoor areas, sports fields, and a dedicated robotics/AI lab.

### Unique Activities
**Play side:**
- Surfing and beach days on the Pacific coast
- Hiking in redwood forests — overnight camping trips
- Beach bonfire nights with stargazing
- Mountain biking on coastal trails
- Beach volleyball tournaments

**Learning side:**
- Robotics and AI lab with industry-grade equipment
- Visits to Silicon Valley tech campuses (guided tours)
- Drone programming and racing in open fields
- 3D printing workshop — design and print your own creations
- Coding sprints with Bay Area tech mentors (guest sessions)

**Cultural:**
- San Francisco city exploration day
- Food truck culture tours
- Street art walks in the Mission District

### Best For
Kids passionate about technology, outdoor adventure, and the startup/innovation mindset. The 3-week duration allows for the deepest project work of any Fizzmind location.

## Cross-Location Details

### Safety & Medical
- All locations have 24/7 medical support on call
- Emergency protocols aligned with local standards
- Background-checked staff at every location
- Parental contact available anytime
- Travel insurance included

### What's Included
- Accommodation (residential, supervised)
- All meals (3 + snacks, dietary needs accommodated)
- All activities and excursions
- Materials and equipment
- Local transportation
- Camp merchandise pack
- End-of-camp portfolio/report

### What's NOT Included
- International flights to/from camp location
- Personal spending money
- Travel insurance (recommended, optional supplement available)
- Airport transfers (available as add-on)

### Parent Communication
- Weekly video update from counselors
- Daily photo/video feed (private, secure platform)
- Emergency contact available 24/7
- End-of-camp comprehensive report with counselor observations
