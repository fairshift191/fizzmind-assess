# Fizzmind — Scholarships

## Our Commitment

Talent doesn't come with a price tag. Fizzmind believes that every gifted, curious kid deserves access to transformative learning experiences — regardless of their family's financial situation. That's why we offer two types of scholarships, and we're serious about both.

---

## Need-Based Scholarships

### Coverage
**Up to 100% of program fees.**

Yes, the full cost. If a family genuinely needs support, we make it work. We would rather have an incredible kid at camp on a full scholarship than an empty spot in the cohort.

### What's Covered
- Full program fees (tuition, accommodation, meals, activities, materials)
- Camp merchandise pack
- End-of-camp portfolio

### What's NOT Covered (even with full scholarship)
- International flights to/from camp location
- Personal spending money
- Travel insurance
- Airport transfers

### Eligibility
- Student must be 10-14 years old
- Student must complete the Fizzmind assessment (voice or test)
- Family demonstrates financial need (documentation required)
- Student shows genuine curiosity and motivation (assessed during application)

### How to Apply
1. Complete the standard Fizzmind assessment (voice conversation or Python test)
2. Select "Apply for Need-Based Scholarship" during registration
3. Parent/guardian submits financial documentation:
   - Recent tax return or income statement
   - Brief letter explaining financial circumstances
   - Any existing financial aid documentation from school
4. Fizzmind Scholarship Committee reviews within 14 days
5. Decision communicated via email with next steps

### Selection Criteria
We evaluate need-based scholarships on:
- **Financial need** (60%) — demonstrated inability to pay full or partial fees
- **Student motivation** (25%) — assessment scores, particularly the "Motivation & Why Us" dimension
- **Diversity contribution** (15%) — geographic, cultural, and background diversity in the cohort

### Partial Need-Based Awards
If full scholarship isn't required, we offer partial need-based support:
- 25% reduction
- 50% reduction
- 75% reduction
- 100% (full scholarship)

The exact amount is determined by the committee based on demonstrated need.

## Merit Scholarships

### Coverage
**Up to 40% of program fees.**

For students who demonstrate exceptional talent, passion, or accomplishment — in ANY area. Not just academics. A kid who's built an incredible robot, written a novel, organized a community project, or mastered an instrument at a competitive level all qualify.

### Eligibility
- Student must be 10-14 years old
- Student must complete the Fizzmind assessment (voice or test)
- Student demonstrates exceptional ability in at least one area

### Merit Tiers

| Tier | Discount | Criteria |
|------|----------|---------|
| Gold | 40% | Exceptional — nationally/internationally recognized achievement, or assessment scores 9+ across multiple dimensions |
| Silver | 25% | Outstanding — significant projects, competitions, or achievements beyond typical for age |
| Bronze | 15% | Notable — clear passion and above-average accomplishment in their area of interest |

### How to Apply
1. Complete the Fizzmind assessment (voice conversation preferred — it captures depth better)
2. Merit scholarship consideration is **automatic** — no separate application needed
3. If the assessment reveals exceptional talent, the Scholarship Committee is notified
4. Optional: parent/student can submit a portfolio of work (projects, awards, videos, code repos, art portfolio)
5. Decision communicated within 7 days of assessment completion

### What Counts as "Merit"
We take a broad, inclusive view of merit. Examples:

**STEM:**
- Built a working robot, app, or AI project
- Won or placed in science fairs, robotics competitions, coding challenges
- Published code on GitHub, app on the App Store
- Self-taught advanced concepts significantly ahead of grade level

**Creative Arts:**
- Portfolio of artwork, music, films, writing
- Performance experience (theater, music, dance)
- Published stories, poems, or articles
- Created and shared creative content online

**Leadership:**
- Organized events, fundraisers, or community projects
- Student council leadership
- Started a club, initiative, or small business
- Mentored or tutored other students

**Sports:**
- Competitive level in any sport
- Team captain or leadership role
- Sportsmanship recognition
- Overcoming significant challenges through athletics

**Global Citizenship:**
- Multilingual proficiency (3+ languages)
- Significant community service
- Cross-cultural experiences or advocacy
- Environmental activism

## Combining Scholarships

Need-based and merit scholarships **can be combined**, up to 100% total coverage.

Example:
- Student qualifies for 50% need-based + 25% merit (Silver) = **75% total scholarship**
- Student qualifies for 75% need-based + 40% merit (Gold) = **100% total** (capped)

## Scholarship Numbers

### Annual Targets
- Minimum 15% of all campers receive some form of scholarship
- At least 5% receive full (100%) scholarships
- No cohort should be entirely full-paying — every group has scholarship kids

### Funding
Scholarships are funded through:
- A portion of program fees from full-paying families
- Corporate partnerships (tech companies, educational foundations)
- Fizzmind Foundation fund (growing annually)
- Individual donors and alumni families

## Scholarship Commitments

### From Fizzmind:
- Scholarship recipients are treated identically to full-paying campers
- No one — not counselors, not other kids — knows who's on scholarship
- All activities, materials, and experiences are the same
- No "scholarship obligations" (no required testimonials, no logo wearing)

### From Scholarship Recipients:
- Complete the full camp program
- Participate fully in all activities
- Treat the opportunity with respect
- Optional (but encouraged): share your experience to inspire others

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Will my kid be treated differently on a scholarship?**
A: Absolutely not. No one at camp knows who's on scholarship. Same room, same food, same activities, same experience.

**Q: Can we apply for scholarship after being accepted?**
A: Yes. Financial circumstances can change. Contact us anytime and we'll work with you.

**Q: Is the scholarship renewable for future camps?**
A: Yes, returning campers can reapply for scholarships each year. Merit tiers may change based on continued growth.

**Q: What if we don't qualify for need-based but can't afford full fees?**
A: Contact us. We offer flexible payment plans (2-4 installments) and can discuss options case by case.

**Q: How competitive are scholarships?**
A: We don't publish acceptance rates, but we fund every qualified need-based application to the extent possible. Merit scholarships are more selective but we award generously.
