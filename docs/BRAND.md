# Fizzmind — Brand Guide

## Name
**fizzmind** (always lowercase in logo/brand usage, "Fizzmind" in sentences)

The name combines **fizz** (energy, excitement, the spark of curiosity bubbling up) with **mind** (intellect, creativity, the thinking brain). Together it captures what we do: ignite young minds through energetic, play-based learning.

## Tagline
**"Where Curiosity Meets Adventure"**

Secondary taglines for specific contexts:
- Marketing: "Learn by playing. Play by building. Build the future."
- For parents: "The camp gifted kids actually want to attend."
- For kids: "Your brain + the world's coolest playground."
- Selective messaging: "20 students. 9 countries. Infinite possibilities."

## Mission
To create the world's most inspiring learning experiences for curious young minds by combining serious play, real-world projects, and global community — all outside the walls of a classroom.

## Vision
A world where every gifted, curious child has access to transformative learning experiences that celebrate who they are and accelerate who they'll become.

## Values

### 1. Curiosity First
Everything starts with a question. We don't hand out answers — we help kids discover them through exploration, experimentation, and play.

### 2. Play Is Serious
We reject the false divide between "fun" and "learning." The most powerful learning happens when kids are so engaged they forget they're learning at all.

### 3. Every Kid Is Remarkable
We look for the spark in every child. Whether they code AI models or draw comic books, build robots or write poetry — we believe every passion has depth worth exploring.

### 4. Global by Nature
Our cohorts bring together kids from around the world. Different cultures, languages, and perspectives aren't just welcomed — they're the whole point.

### 5. Selective, Not Exclusive
We curate cohorts of 20 because quality matters. But we also offer scholarships up to 100% because talent doesn't come with a price tag.

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Warm but not soft** — we're encouraging, not patronizing
- **Smart but not academic** — we know our stuff but we don't lecture
- **Playful but not silly** — we have fun but we're serious about impact
- **Confident but not arrogant** — we're selective and proud of it, but never elitist

### Writing Rules
- Use "kids" not "students" (except in formal contexts)
- Use "build" and "create" not "learn" and "study"
- Use "adventure" and "challenge" not "program" and "curriculum"
- Never say "classroom", "homework", "grades", or "test scores"
- Speak TO kids, not ABOUT them — they're our audience too
- Short sentences. Active voice. No jargon.

### Examples

**Good:** "You'll spend your mornings building robots and your afternoons kayaking with kids from six different countries."

**Bad:** "Our STEM curriculum incorporates hands-on robotics modules alongside structured aquatic recreation activities."

**Good:** "We don't accept everyone. That's what makes it special."

**Bad:** "Our rigorous admissions process ensures optimal cohort composition."

## Typography

### Headings
**Space Grotesk** — Bold, modern geometric sans-serif
- Logo/brand name: 700 weight, lowercase, letter-spacing: -0.04em
- Section headings: 700 weight, -0.02em tracking
- Available weights: 400, 500, 600, 700

### Body
**Inter** — Clean, highly legible humanist sans-serif
- Body text: 300 weight (light)
- Labels: 500-600 weight, uppercase, wide tracking
- Monospace context (code): Space Grotesk at 400 weight

### Scale
| Element | Size | Weight |
|---------|------|--------|
| Brand name (hero) | 72px | 700 |
| Page title | 32-48px | 700 |
| Section heading | 22-28px | 700 |
| Card title | 16-22px | 600 |
| Body text | 14-16px | 300 |
| Label | 11-13px | 600, uppercase |
| Caption | 11-12px | 400-500 |

## Logo Usage

The Fizzmind logo is the word "fizzmind" set in Space Grotesk Bold, always lowercase.

### Rules
- Always lowercase: `fizzmind`, never "FizzMind" or "FIZZMIND"
- Minimum clear space: 1x the height of the "f" on all sides
- On dark backgrounds: white text, optional gold dot on the "i"
- On light backgrounds: dark text (#06060B), gold dot on the "i"
- Never stretch, rotate, or add effects
- Never place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds

### The Dot
The dot on the "i" in fizzmind can optionally be rendered in Fizzmind Gold (#D4A853) as a brand accent. This is the primary distinguishing mark.

## Imagery Guidelines

### Photography Style
- Real kids doing real things (not stock photo smiles)
- Natural lighting, authentic moments
- Diversity in every image — multiple ethnicities, cultures, body types
- Action shots > posed shots
- Show the MIX: coding + hiking, robots + campfires

### Illustration Style
- Minimal, geometric, dark background
- Gold and blue accent colors
- Abstract representations of concepts (not cartoon characters)
- Clean lines, generous whitespace
